archive

In collaboration with members and management of Keane, I led the creative direction for the 20th anniversary of their debut album, Hopes and Fears. Access to the band’s archive meant that a small team of designers and I were able to reimagine the album, while staying true to the artwork and photography that defined its initial release.

outcome

We explored a range of creative avenues; from a complete redesign inspired by original graphics and sketches, to a more modest update that highlighted the significance of the anniversary. What began as small conversations with the band ultimately led to a comprehensive brand identity, inspiring everything from the album artwork, to merchandise and advertising.
